# Trademark Policy

## Overview

This document outlines the trademark policy for **ZeroBuild**, an open-source autonomous software factory developed by PotLock.

## Trademarks

The following trademarks are owned by PotLock:

- **ZeroBuild**™ - The primary project name
- **ZeroBuild Logo** - The official project logo and visual identity
- **PotLock**™ - The organization name

## Permitted Uses

You may use the ZeroBuild trademarks without prior written permission in the following contexts:

### 1. Referring to the Project
- Accurately describing your software's compatibility with ZeroBuild
- Linking to the official ZeroBuild repository
- Referencing ZeroBuild in documentation, articles, or presentations
- Describing forks or derivatives in a way that does not imply official status

### 2. Non-Commercial Uses
- Educational materials and tutorials
- Conference presentations and talks
- Blog posts and technical articles
- Open source projects that build upon or integrate with ZeroBuild

### 3. Modified Versions
- Forks of the codebase for your own use or modification
- Must clearly distinguish from the official ZeroBuild project
- Must not imply endorsement by PotLock

## Prohibited Uses

You may NOT use ZeroBuild trademarks:

### 1. Impersonation
- In a way that implies affiliation with or endorsement by PotLock
- As part of your company name, product name, or service name
- In domain names, social media handles, or app stores without permission

### 2. Confusion
- In connection with software that is not compatible with ZeroBuild
- To distribute modified versions without clear differentiation
- In a manner that could confuse users about the source of the software

### 3. Commercial Exploitation
- Selling merchandise (t-shirts, stickers, etc.) without written permission
- Using in advertising without clear attribution
- Claiming ownership of the ZeroBuild trademarks

## Guidelines for Proper Use

When using ZeroBuild trademarks:

1. **Attribution**: Include attribution like "ZeroBuild is a trademark of PotLock"
2. **Accuracy**: Ensure your use accurately describes your relationship to ZeroBuild
3. **Distinction**: Clearly distinguish your project from the official ZeroBuild project
4. **No Modification**: Do not modify the logos or stylized text except for scaling

## Forks and Derivatives

If you create a fork or derivative work:

- You must remove all ZeroBuild and PotLock branding
- You must choose a new name for your project
- You must not use any confusingly similar names
- You should acknowledge the original project in your README

Example proper attribution:
```
This project is based on ZeroBuild by PotLock.
ZeroBuild is licensed under MIT/Apache-2.0.
```

## Logo Usage

The ZeroBuild logo may be used:
- ✅ In presentations and educational materials about ZeroBuild
- ✅ In blog posts and articles discussing ZeroBuild
- ✅ In project READMEs when linking to or referencing ZeroBuild

The ZeroBuild logo may NOT be used:
- ❌ As your project or company logo
- ❌ Modified or altered in any way
- ❌ In connection with incompatible or competing products
